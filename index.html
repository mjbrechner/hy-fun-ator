26 February 2024: Added a "no dash, no space" option.

3 August 2023: Added an alternate version of the hyphen found in Unicode. The most common hyphen, referred to as the "hyphen-minus," appears on most keyboards and is used for a minus sign, dash, or hyphen. This version of the hyphen has been with the Hy-fun-ator since the beginning, but has now been renamed "Hyphen-minus."

A different version of hyphen, used in Unicode, appears on occassion, though this is just a version of the standard hyphen. Despite looking virtually identical to the hyphen-minus, it is treated as a unique character. This update adds functionality for this character to the Hy-fun-ator as Hyphen (Unicode).
