Putting the fun in hyphenation

Want to see your hyphenated text with all kinds of possible dashes? Type something in the box using the standard hyphen. After clicking the button, your hyphens should transform to match various kinds of dashes.

No dash

hy fun ator

Hyphen

hy-fun-ator

Figure dash

hy‒fun‒ator

En dash

hy–fun–ator

Quotation dash

hy―fun―ator

Em dash

hy—fun—ator
